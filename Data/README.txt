Data base for Rao et al. (2021) Fisheries induced shifts in sea snake assemblages along the Konkan coast of India.

Email: shawn27.dsouza@gmail.com
Collection: Jan 2017 - May 2019
Location: Malvan, Maharashtra

India-States.*: Shape files for the Indian landmass. (Source: DivaGIS)

Rao et al_data_sea snake bycatch.csv: Sea snake bycatch data from Trawlers, shore seines (Rampan) and gillnets
  - Sr.No.: Serial number of the observation
  - Date: Date of sampling
  - Field.Code: Unique indentifier for sampled individual
  - Species: Specific identity of the sampled individuals
  - Location.encountered: Landing location of fishing boat sampled
  - Gear.Type: Gear employed by the boat for fishing trip
  - Boat.Name.Owner: ID for sampled boat
  - No..of.Hauls: NUmbe rof times the net was set or hauled during the fishing trip
  - Average.Haul.Duration..Hours.: Mean time the net was set or hauled during the fishing trip
  - Condition.at.encounter.: Condition of the sea snake when it was taken from the net (Dead or alive)
  - Snout.to.Vent..cm.: Snout to vent length of the individual in cms
  - Tail.Lenght..cm: Tail length of the individual in cms
  - Weight..g.: Weight of the whole snake in grams
  - Sex: Sex of the snake determined by palpation or dissection
  - Remarks:
  
Rao et al_data_Past Studies.csv: Data compiled from Lobo (2004) and Padate et al. (2009)
  - Study: Cited literature
  - Year: Year the study was conducted
  - Species: : Species rank of sea snake
  - Total.Abn: Abundance of sea snake reported in study
  - Total.fe: Fishing effort reported in study
  - n.alive: Number of live sea snakes encountered
  - n.dead: Number of dead sea snakes encountered
  - mort.rate: Mortality rate of sea snakes encountered
  - prop.females: Proportion of female sea snakes encountered
  - Remarks

Rao et al_data_state-craft-gear.csv: Compiled data on fishing pressure in the Konkan region
  - Year: Year of reporting
  - State: State of reporting
  - Craft: Type of fishing boat
  - n: Number of active fishing boats
  - Reference
  - Remarks

Rao et al_data_study_site.csv: Locations sampled by Lobo (2004) and Padate et al. (2009)

Rao et al_data_study_periods.csv: Years of sampling by Lobo (2004) and Padate et al. (2009)
